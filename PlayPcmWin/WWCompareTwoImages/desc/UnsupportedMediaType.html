Error: Unsupported Media Type.

If this error happens on video read, it may be fixed by installing video decoder to your computer.

  1. Open Windows File Explorer.
  2. Navigate to the video file to show, right click the video file and "Open With" > "Movies & TV"
  3. Movies & TV app starts and the same error will occur, but on the screen, there may be video decoder install option is shown. Follow the instruction to download video decoder.
  4. After video decoder installation, confirm Movies & TV app now can decode video. If you are lucky WWCompareTwoImages is also able to decode the video.

App will exit.
